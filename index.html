About Me

I am a graduate student in Plant Pathology at North Dakota State University. My research focuses on soybean cyst nematode, host resistance, plant breeding, and molecular approaches for disease management.

Research

My current work includes evaluation of soybean and dry bean germplasm for resistance to soybean cyst nematode, Rhg1 copy number variation analysis, and comparison of field and controlled-environment screening methods.

Skills

SAS, R, Python, qPCR, statistical analysis, genomics, bioinformatics, plant disease research.
