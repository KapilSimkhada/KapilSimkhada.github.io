About Me

I am an M.S. student in Plant Pathology at North Dakota State University. My research focuses on soybean cyst nematode resistance, Rhg1 copy number variation, dry bean and soybean germplasm evaluation, and molecular tools for crop improvement.

Research Interests

Soybean Cyst Nematode

Screening soybean and dry bean germplasm for resistance to important SCN populations.

Rhg1 CNV Analysis

Studying copy number variation and its relationship with phenotypic resistance.

Field and Growth Chamber Studies

Comparing field performance with controlled-environment screening methods.

Data Analysis

Using SAS, R, and Python for experimental analysis, visualization, and interpretation.

Molecular Tools

Data & Statistics

SAS, R, Python, ANOVA, correlation analysis, graphical visualization.
