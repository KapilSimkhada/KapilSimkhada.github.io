About Me

Graduate student in Plant Pathology at NDSU working on soybean cyst nematode (SCN) resistance in soybean and dry bean.

Research

Working on host resistance to soybean cyst nematode (SCN) in both soybean and dry bean. In soybean, conducting resistance screening and evaluating Rhg1 copy number variation (CNV) in breeding lines, along with developing KASP markers for Rhg4-based resistance to support marker-assisted selection (MAS). In dry bean, performing both field and controlled (growth chamber) experiments to assess resistance responses.

Skills

SAS, R, Python, qPCR, statistical analysis, genomics, bioinformatics, plant disease research.
